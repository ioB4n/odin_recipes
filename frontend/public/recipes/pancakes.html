Pancakes


Description

These are called "The Rock's Special". This pancake recipe is delicious and will get you ripped as hell.


Ingredients

  • 2 egg
  • 2 bananas
  • 2 scoops of protein powder
  • 300ml whole milk
  • 6 scoops of oat flour

Steps

  1. Wake up
  2. Crush the bananas in a bowl
  3. Add the eggs and mix them together
  4. Add the protein powder and mix everything together
  5. Add the milk
  6. Add the oat flour and mix
  7. Jump off
