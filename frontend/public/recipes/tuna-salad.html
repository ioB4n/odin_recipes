Tuna Salad


Description

Healthy and high in protein. Careful with the fish tho, cause you might lose an inch or so.


Ingredients

  • Carrot
  • Cucumber
  • Cabbage
  • Canned tuna

Steps

  1. Shred some carrot
  2. Cut the cuc into small pieces
  3. Cut cabbage in thin slices
  4. Put tuna in and mix everything together
